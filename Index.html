George Washington

Term: 1789-1797

YOL: 1732-1799

VP: John Adams

Quote: "If the freedom of speech is taken away then dumb and silent we may be led, like sheep to the slaughter."

Washington did not Chop Down a Cherry Tree. This was a Myth created in a 19th Century Book to show how honest Washington had been.

John Adams

Term: 1797-1801

YOL: 1735-1826

VP: Col. Thomas Jefferson

Quote: "Facts are stubborn things; and whatever may be our wishes, our inclinations, or the dictates of our passions, they cannot alter the state of facts and evidence."

Before He Died, Adams said "At Least Jefferson Lives". Adams did not know that Jefferson had died hours earlier. Coincidence or Not, They died on the 50th 4th of July.

Col. Thomas Jefferson

Term: 1801-1809

YOL: 1743-1826

VP: Lt. Col. Aaron Burr, Brig. Gen. George Clinton

Quote: "Honesty is the first chapter in the book of wisdom."

Jefferson soaked his feet in cold water everyday. He claimed it improved his health.

Col. James Madison

Term:1809-1817

YOL: 1751-1836

VP: Brig. Gen. George Clinton, Elbridge Gerry

Quote: "The essence of Government is power; and power, lodged as it must be in human hands, will ever be liable to abuse."

Madison was our shortest president at 5'4. He was often seen 3 in. below his wife. Unusual in the 18th and 19th centurys

Maj. James Monroe

Term: 1817-1825

YOL: 1758-1831

VP: Daniel Tompkins

Quote: "The best form of government is that which is most likely to prevent the greatest sum of evil."

In 1823, Monroe prevented further colinization by Europe in North America. He proclaimed this in the Monroe Doctrine(Author: John Quincy Adams).

John Quincy Adams

Term: 1825-1829

YOL: 1767-1848

VP: John Calhoun

Quote: "If your actions inspire others to dream more, learn more, do more and become more, you are a leader."

Once when he was swimming(naked in the Potomac River), a news reporter came and sat on the rock with his clothes on it and required an interview before she would leave.

Maj. Gen. Andrew Jackson

Term: 1829-1837

YOL: 1767-1845

VP: John Calhoun, Martin Van Buren

Quote: "One man with courage makes a majority."

Jackson was responsible for the 16,000 casulties in the Trail of Tears.

Martin Van Buren

Term: 1837-1841

YOL: 1782-1862

VP:Col. Richard Johnson

Quote: "It is better to do a job right than to explain why you didnt."

Martin Van Buren was the first President born a US Citizen not a British Subject.

Maj. Gen. William Harrison

Term: 1841

YOL: 1773-1841

VP: Capt. John Tyler

Quote: "There is nothing more corrupting, nothing more destructive of the noblest and finest feelings of our nature, than the exercise of unlimited power."

During his inaugural address, Harrison did not wear winter appropriate clothes. Harrison caught pneumonia.Harrison developed diarreha from which he died.

Capt. John Tyler

Term: 1841-1845

YOL: 1790-1862

VP:None

Quote: "Wealth can only be accumulated by the earnings of industry and frugality"

Tyler was the only former President not honored on his birthday. Abraham Lincoln did not honor him because he was elected to the Confederate States House of Representatives

Pvt. James Polk

Term: 1845-1849

YOL: 1795-1849

VP: George Dallas

Quote: "No president who performs his duties faithfully and conscientiously can have any leisure."

Polk, as president, bought the most land. Exceeding the Louisiana Purchase by 5,520,362 mi.

Brig. Gen. David Atchison

Term: 1849

YOL: 1807-1886

VP: John Clayton

Since Zachary Taylor didnt want to be sworn in on Sunday, Taylor picked the President Pro Tempre of the Senate David Atchison. Atchison was President Sunday March 04, 1849.

Maj. Gen. Zachary Taylor

Term: 1849-1850

YOL: 1784-1850

VP: Maj. Millard Fillmore

Quote: "I have always done my duty. I am ready to die. My only regret for me is the friends I left behind."

Taylor died of Hot Milk and Cherries he ate on the 4th of July

Maj. Millard Fillmore

Term: 1850-1853

YOL: 1800-1874

VP: None

Quote: "A honorable defeat is better than a dishonorable victory."

Fillmore was the oldest student at 19 of his future wife's class at New Hope Acadamy.

Brig. Gen. Franklin Pierce

Term: 1853-1857

YOL: 1804-1869

VP: William King

Quote: "If your past is limited, your future is boundless "

In 1852, Pierce beat his old General, General Winfield Scott in the Presidential Election.

Pvt. James Buchanan

Term: 1857-1861

YOL: 1791-1868

VP: John Breckinridge

Quote: "The test of leadership is not to put greatness into humanity, but to elicit it, for the greatness is already there."

James Buchanan had 1 fiance, she died before they could get married.

Capt. Abraham Lincoln

Term: 1861- 1865

YOL: 1809-1865

VP: Hannibal Hamlin, Andrew Johnson

Quote: "Government of the people, by the people, for the people, shall not perish from the Earth."

Abraham Lincoln was the tallest President at 6'4

Brig. Gen. Andrew Johnson

Term: 1865-1869

YOL: 1808-1875

VP: None

Quote: "Honest conviction is my courage; the Constitution is my guide."

Johnson was the 1st President to be put on Impeachment Trial. However, Johnson was impeached the other wasnt. Johnson was Impeached on racial charges.

Gen. Hiram Grant

Term: 1869-1977

YOL: h3> VP: Schuyler Colfax, Henry Wilson

Quote: "I have never advocated war except as a means of peace."

Grant hated his initials HUG. When he joined the army, the recruiter got his name wrong as Ulysses Simpson Grant. Grant didnt correct him. Grant never leagally changed his name so it remains Hiram Ulysses Grant.

Brev. Maj. Gen. Rutherford Hayes

Term: 1877-1881

YOL:

VP: William Wheeler

Quote: "The President of the United States should strive to be always mindful of the fact that he serves his party best who serves his country best."

When Hayes visited San Francisco, he became the first president to go to the west coast while in office.

Maj. Gen. James Garfield

Term: 1881

YOL: -1881

VP: Chester Arthur

Quote: "The truth will set you free, but first it will make you miserable"

Garfield was shot in July 1881 and died in September 1881 of Lead Poisoning

Brig. Gen. Chester Arthur

Term: 1881-1885

YOL: -1885

VP: None

Quote: "Men may die, but the fabrics of free institutions remains unshaken."

Arthur had a kidney disease of which he told nobody and died months out of office.

Stephen Cleveland

Term: 1885-1889, 1893-1897

YOL: 1837-1908

VP: Thomas Hendricks, Adlai Stevenson I

Quote: "Communism is a hateful thing, and a menace to peace and organized government."

Cleveland is the only president to serve 2 non-consecutive terms.

Brig. Gen. Benjamin Harrison

Term: 1889-1893

YOL: 1833-1901

VP: Levi Morton

Quote: "Great lives never go out; they go on"

Harrison's great grandfather, Benjamin Harrison V, signed the Declaration of Independence. Harrison's grandfather was President Maj. Gen. William Harrison, Hero of Tippacanoe.
